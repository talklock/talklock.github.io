Our Goal

Bike theft is a constant problem faced by cyclists across Canada. Daily commuters in particular struggle to find inexpensive, secure ways to keep their bikes safe. TALK Design has chosen to create an affordable way for daily bicycle commuters to securely lock their bikes.

Our solution is an intelligent bike rack with a built-in locking system. Users must simply carry a card with them that they can use to lock their bike, and unlock their bike whe they return.

Possible applications are for municipalities, campuses, companies, etc. to buy these stations and place them in bicycle hubs such as subway stations and bus terminals.

Our Product

The BLOCK

Using the BLOCK is easy! All you have to do is pull up with your bike, tap your WatCard, wrap the chain around your bike, and insert the tab into the receptacle. Our indicator lights and clear instructions make the process intuitive and quick.

Interface

Our system uses WatCard's built-in Near Field Communication (NFC) for convenient and secure user verification. Simply tap your WatCard to lock up your bike, and tap it when you come back to unlock it. The encrypted, short distance verification of NFC prevents your ride from being taken away by anyone other than you.

Locking System

While reliable chain locks are too heavy to be portable, a stationary locking system allows for a sufficiently strong chain to protect your bike from theft. Our system holds up against a powerful attack from a crowbar or bolt cutters, while maintaining the affordable and convenient ride that daily commuters need.
